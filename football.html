what is soccer?

Football is a team sport in which two teams of 11 players compete, each trying to score goals against the other team.Football is one of the most popular sports in the world,and is widely followed by the masses.

Football history

Football dates back to the Middle Ages in Europe,when it was played between different villages.The first football club was established in England in 1863,and since then the sport has evolved and spread all over the world.Many local and intermational tournaments are held in football,including the World Cup,the European Champoins League, and local leagues in many countries.

football rules

Football is characterized by strong competition between teams and playing tactics,and it contains a number of rules and regulations that players must abide by during the match.Among the most important of these rules are:

  • Football is played on a rectagular field with specific measurements.
  • The field is divided into two halves,and each team tries to score goals in the other team's goal.
  • The foot is used to play the ball,no hands or arms are allowed,except for the goaldeeper in some cases.
  • Players must adhere to fair play and not resort to violence or unsportsmanlike aggression.
  • There are some regulations for substitutions,yellow and red cards,etc
